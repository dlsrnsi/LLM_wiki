## Comparative Summary

The overview layer is where individual paper notes become a connected research map. The current overview compares model families across the wiki: network-based DTI, CNN sequence DTI, binding-region-aware transformers, virtual screening, AMP classification, hERG safety, and broad review context.

Compared with category pages, overview pages should answer cross-cutting questions:

- How do model inputs evolve from hand-crafted descriptors to learned sequence and graph representations?
- Which papers produce actionable wet-lab candidates?
- Which papers emphasize interpretability, and what kind?
- Where are the current gaps in the drug discovery pipeline?
